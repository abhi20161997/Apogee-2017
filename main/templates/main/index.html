      CARE: Campaign for Acid Regulation

      About The Campaign

      CARE is a campaign that aims to put an end to the horrendous acid attacks happening in the country. The number of acid attacks are escalating with each day. Each year, hundreds of acid attacks are reported all over India. Will you let a cheap bottle of acid disrupt your life and shatter your dreams? The answer is - No. This petition aims at reshaping lives of such victims. Supreme Court gave guidelines in 2013 on having a check on the sale of acid. However, no laws have been formulated on the basis of those guidelines, resulting in an uncontrolled sale of acid in open market. This campaign aims at getting a minimum of 50,000 signatures by APOGEE and urge the government to take initiatives on this matter. Submitting this petition to the Home Minister and Minister of Women and Child Development will ensure that our voices against this injustice reach the parliament and hopefully, the guidelines will be enforced as laws very soon.
      This campaign also aims to increase awareness about these attacks and their effects among the citizens. The general public should be educated regarding the use of acid and precautions to be taken. The petition intends to bring the knowledge of misuse of acid and its consequences to the people.

      The Statistics

      Almost 500 cases of acid attacks were reported in India the previous year and many more go unreported because the attacker is generally a known person to the victim, and hence, the victim is reluctant to report it. Around 85% of these victims are young women. 40% are attacked in public while 37% are attacked at home. Studies show that 34% of cases are because of the refusal in sexual advances or marriages.

      Faq

      Accommodation Charges

      Accommodation to the guest would be provided in the campus of BITS Pilani and it will be purely on a shared basis. These facilities would be separate for girls and boys.

      There are no accommodation charges. This does not cover meals though.

      Accommodation facility would be made available from 4 PM on 22nd of March, 2017 onwards and you would be required to vacate the room on or before 8 AM on 27th March, 2017.

      Caution Money Policy

      Refund Procedure: When you wish to checkout, report to the hospitality desk along with the accommodation receipt for refund procedure.The commodities provided to you by APOGEE will be verified at your accommodation , after which you can collect your refund.

      Will all the team members be given accommodation at the same place?

      We will try but there is no surety for the same.

      What kind of accommodation is provided?

      Accommodation is provided on a shared basis inside campus hostels. One mattress, and an enough space for keeping luggage, and other essential commodities will be provided. Girls and boys will be accommodated separately. Number of guest in a room will be decided by APOGEE and will be allotted by APOGEE team.

      Does the accommodation include food facilities too?

      No, the accommodation does not include food facilities. Guest can purchase their meals from the food court, night cafeteria or private hostel canteens or hostel messes

      Do we get any food facilities at outside accommodation places?

      You can purchase your meals from the hotels or the nearby restaurants.

      What are the food facilities inside the campus?

      For all our guests and guest we set up a huge cafeteria serving a variety of cuisines satisfying the needs of every palate. In addition, you can also eat in the many canteens, messes, and restaurant inside the campus.

      What about the hospital facility?

      There is an institute Hospital located near the Gym G. In case you fall ill, you are advised to report to us at accommodation desk. We shall make appropriate arrangements for you to be treated at the Institute hospital. We shall also be carrying a first aid kit with us, at the accommodation desk.

      What about the security facilities?

      BITS campus has a vigilant and round-the-clock security service. To ensure the safety of the participants, there will be additional security guards in hostels in order to avoid thefts and other mishaps. Although APOGEE team will not take responsibility for any theft or other mishaps. So guest are requested to take care of their luggage and valuable items.
