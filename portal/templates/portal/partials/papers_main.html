Paper Abstract Submission

The deadline for submission of Paper Abstract is:
BITSian Participants: 14th November, 2016 11:59pm.
Non-BITSian Participants (Outstation): 20th February, 2017 11:59pm.

Non-BITSian can also directly mail their final paper entries to pep@bits-apogee.org
INSTRUCTIONS :
  • The results for the abstracts will be announced via E-Mail and on the Facebook Page.
  • Participants have to upload their abstracts for screening in the appropriate format on the Apogee 2017 portal.The portal will be accessible from 26th October 2016. The prescribed format must be followed, failing which may lead to disqualification of the participants.
  • The abstract should be submitted only in the format provided below.
  • Only genuine and original paper abstracts should be uploaded. Abstracts found copied from any source like internet/ journals etc. will be disqualified.
  • Only undergraduate and postgraduate students of any recognized university can participate.
  • A maximum of 2 students per team are allowed to present a paper (one as author and another as co-author). Participants from different institutions can also form a team.
  • The abstract will be judged on the basis of their innovation and knowledge of the field.
  • Plagiarized content will be strictly dealt with and would lead to participant's disqualification. In case of any discrepancy, organizers decision will be final and binding.
  • Participants can't submit one paper in multiple categories. Also, participants can present more than one paper. However, the abstracts should be submitted separately.
