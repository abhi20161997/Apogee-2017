IC ENGINE WORKSHOP 25-26 10am onwards
Location 6151
Internal Combustion or IC Engines have had a great impact on human life. These engines produce immense power in contrast to their size and weight. Today, these engines power almost all air and land vehicles.In this workshop students get firsthand practical experience of the working principles for IC Engine Design . Participants can explore the engineering involved in an automotive engine system.
We have actual IC Engines to experiment with during the workshop.
